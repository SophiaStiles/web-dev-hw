French Toast is a Classic

        French toast is my favorite breakfast food becuase it is so versital.
        French toast can be savory by diping it into a batter of spices and eggs and topping it off with peppers and mushrooms with hot sauce.
        It can also be sweet by dipping it into an egg batter and topping it with syrup or bearries and cream.
        It is something so simple to make pretty quick and so delicious. What is better than waking up on a Sunday morning with your loved ones
        and sitting around a nice meal together? Honestly not much, so for you next Sunday breakfast try this french toast recipe.
