French Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 8 slices of either challah, brioche, or white bread (Please refer to desciptions to see the difference between bread types)
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup,warmed

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, combine cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar and set aside briefly.
  2. In a 10-inch or 12-inch skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Whisk together cinnamon mixture, eggs, milk, and vanilla and pour into a shallow container such as a pie plate.
  4. Dip bread in egg mixture. Fry slices until golden brown, then flip to cook the other side. Serve with syrup.














Bread Type Outcome Description
Brioche Perfect thickness for french toast. Will be flavorful with crisp outside and a softer middle.
Challah An eggy bread pairs perfectly. Expect thicker french toast that is soft in center and crisp on outside.
White Bread Most common at restaurants. Expect a very thin french toast. Can get eggy if soaked for too long.
